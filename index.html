About Maserati MC20

The Maserati MC20 is a supercar that marks Maserati’s bold return to performance. With a twin-turbo V6 engine producing 621 horsepower, the MC20 accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in under 3 seconds. Built with a lightweight carbon-fiber frame, it offers both racing precision and everyday comfort.
